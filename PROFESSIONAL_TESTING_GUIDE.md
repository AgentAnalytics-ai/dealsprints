## ‚úÖ Quality Scoring Criteria

**100% Score = Excellent:**
- Original insight (not a rewrite)
- OKC-specific location mentioned
- Business/opportunity context
- Legal source
- Complete data

**80-99% = Good:**
- Mostly original
- Some specific details
- Minor improvements needed

**60-79% = Needs Improvement:**
- Generic content
- Missing business context
- Could be more specific

**<60% = Poor:**
- May be a rewrite
- Missing key information
- Copyrighted source

## üîç What to Look For

### ‚úÖ Good Content Should:
- Mention specific OKC neighborhoods/locations
- Focus on business implications/opportunities
- Be original (not a rewrite)
- Come from legal sources
- Be 2-3 sentences, conversational tone

### ‚ùå Red Flags:
- "According to..." (sounds like rewrite)
- Generic "Oklahoma City, OK" (not specific)
- No business context
- From copyrighted sources
- Too short or too generic
